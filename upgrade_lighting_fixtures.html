Upgrade Lighting Fixtures

Good lighting can instantly elevate the ambiance of a room. Replace outdated fixtures with modern, energy-efficient alternatives to brighten up your space. Consider installing dimmer switches to adjust the intensity of light according to your mood and activities. Incorporate a variety of lighting sources, including overhead fixtures, table lamps, and sconces, to create layers of light and add depth to your interior design.
